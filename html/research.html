Research and References

We have taken references from various websites while creating this project. We took ideas and concepts from these sites to make our webpage more attractive and efficient.

Reference 1.

Care section of Home page was inspired from the above given website Bonsai Empire. Bonsai Empire's care section describes how to take care of bonsai plants. This website helped us in elaborating about different species of Bonsai having different characters and different nature of survival.

Reference 2.

Products page was inspired by the Brussel Bonsai's shop all page. The products in our website are displayed in the similar layout. Only names and prices of the products are displayed in both the pages.

Reference 3.

Blog page has taken reference from Brainspire. The layout of both the pages are similar. Both the pages have heading in bold and in larger font-size comparatively to other parts of the website. The images are centred in both the sites.

Reference 4.

Blog page, again takes reference from the above shown page Brainspire for the content of the blog. The heading layout and its color has been implemented in our Blog with the help of CSS font-color property.

Reference 5.

About us was inspired from beaver builder's templates. It has a similar layout and gave an idea about a simple and clean looking site. Color palettes were chosen from color hunts and few references were taken from w3schools and google.
