What is technology?

Technology is a powerful tool which applies scientific knowledge to practical aims of human life to improve productivity, creativity and convenience. The world is so immersed in it that it’s hard to believe that even 20 years ago things were extremely different. It is sometimes referred to, the alteration and manipulation of the human environment. Our ability to apply scientific knowledge is made possible by technology. It encompasses not only devices, such as computers, but also methods and procedures, such as the method by which computer chips are manufactured. The goal of information technology is to facilitate tasks.

Uses of technology in daily life

It’s quite fascinating how technology has changed our daily lives. We use technology every passing day by one means or another. What blows my mind is how technology has evolved with the changing time and needs. Not long ago, we used diaries for our assignments. Platforms such as Google Classroom and My Second Teacher are modern diaries which have helped save us paper, environment and our time. Similarly, technology has made it easier to farm, more feasible to build cities, and more convenient to travel, among many other things, effectively helping to create globalisation, and making it easier for economies to grow and for companies to do business.

Positive Impacts Of Technology

The world of technology is ever-evolving, and many fields have been completely transformed by technological breakthroughs and advancements. Let’s have a look at the positive impacts technology has made in our society.

1. Easy communication

Who hasn’t heard of facebook, instagram or twitter? They have become our primary source of communication today. With a click of a button, you can make calls, send emails and do a lot of things with the help of technology. Now you don’t need to write letters to your loved ones if you are missing them. Do a video call and feel them close to you. In addition to social media platforms, there are numerous other applications specifically designed for communication. If social media isn’t for you, WhatsApp and other messaging apps enable you to quickly message family and friends and even make calls over Wi-Fi. While the 2020 pandemic had kept everyone socially apart, technology allowed families, friends and relatives to connect virtually. Nepal’s family is one of many who celebrated Dashain Tika online during the lockdown.

2. Advanced health care

Many technological advances have eased patient care, as well as patient comfort.Patients now have access to prosthetics, implants, hearing aids, dental devices whereas earlier, these types of accommodations used to be expensive and difficult to make. Furthermore, the collection of an extremely large pool of information related to health and medicine can further the accuracy of analytical research, predict health trends and major epidemics, and ultimately speed up the search for disease prevention and eradication.
Another big perk of health care advancements in technology is the ability to store and transmit health care records easily and efficiently. Gone are the days of bulky paper files that have to be faxed to other medical providers.This shift to digital records not only streamlines the documentation process itself, but reduces costs and improves patient-doctor relationships, as patients get easy access to their health records via an online interface. There are also personalised applications available to help patients improve anxiety and stress, monitor sleep, get therapy without having to go to a physical location, monitor and track their diet and exercise, and more.
New technologies such as virtual visits, chatbots were being extensively used to deliver healthcare to individuals, especially during Covid-19.

3. Industrialization of agriculture

Agricultural societies were previously based on the cycle of the seasons.Agriculture workers laboured six days a week, from sunup to sun down, just to keep their crops growing. Although conditions like these still exist, the industrialization of agriculture radically transformed due to technological advancements.The innovation of machinery such as combined harvesters and planters simplifies the task and reduces the cost of labour.
The farmers cannot determine the sowing period, but with the aid of Artificial Intelligence farmers can know the weather changes by the use of weather forecasts.
Satellites, drones and aerial imaging are becoming advanced in taking the farm high quality images. This equipment’s aid the farmers to analyse the crops while they are at the comfort of their home as if they are actually in the farm. They access the crops' status from a distance. The drones can also be used to spray pesticides on the crops.

4. Educational achievements

Google search, online courses and youtube videos have allowed people to have faster access to education.Technology has a positive impact on learning. Technology provides hands-on learning opportunities to integrate into all school curriculum areas including Mathematics, Reading, Science and Social Studies, and other departments.
It provides an opportunity to collaborate with peers and learn from each other. The rapid development of technology is changing the way students and teachers think. So with advanced and fast growing technology they get very creative. The accessibility of technology has made it easier for us to experience so many things, making us even more productive and creative. One of the most significant benefits of technology in education is that it has made information more accessible to all.
Not only does it facilitate access to information, it also facilitates access to updated information.
